About

I am a Master's student in the Mathematics in Data Science program at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), where I also work as a Graduate Research Student in the Computer Vision Group under Prof. Daniel Cremers and Dr. Héctor Andrade Loarca. Previously, I completed double Bachelor's degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science at Heidelberg University.

My research interests sit at the intersection of mathematical computer vision, deep learning theory, and scientific computing. I am particularly focused on hardware-aware neural rendering, generative 3D modeling, low-level GPU acceleration (CUDA), and the mathematical foundations of deep learning.
